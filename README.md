MagnifyAll: Universal Product Reader

Short Description:
Scan any barcode to instantly read ingredients, warnings, and product details in massive, easy-to-read text. Your smart magnifying glass for tiny labels!

About the App

Have you ever struggled to read the microscopic ingredients list on a food wrapper, the warnings on a medicine bottle, or the chemical contents of your cleaning supplies? MagnifyAll is designed specifically to solve this problem.

Built with accessibility in mind, MagnifyAll acts as a universal smart-magnifier. Simply point your camera at a product's barcode, and the app instantly searches global databases to pull up the product's information, presenting it to you in a clean, high-contrast, and massively magnified text format.

Whether you are a senior citizen, someone with visual impairments, an allergy sufferer, or just someone who wants to know exactly what is in the products you buy, MagnifyAll makes reading the fine print effortless.

Key Features

📸 Instant Barcode Scanner: Uses your device's camera to quickly scan traditional 1D barcodes.

🔍 Adjustable Massive Text: Zoom in and out with dedicated, easy-to-tap buttons to make the text as large as you need it to be (up to 54px font size).

🗂️ Universal Database Search: A powerful "waterfall" search engine that automatically checks multiple global databases:

Food Items: (Powered by Open Food Facts)

Over-The-Counter Medicine: (Powered by the U.S. FDA)

Beauty & Personal Care: Cosmetics, soaps, and shampoos (Powered by Open Beauty Facts)

General Products: Cleaning supplies and household goods (Powered by Open Products Facts)

⌨️ Manual Search Fallback: Barcode won't scan? Easily type in the barcode number or the brand name (e.g., "Tylenol") to pull up the exact same large-print information.

⚠️ Allergen & Warning Alerts: Automatically highlights known allergens in food and crucial medical warnings in distinct, color-coded alert boxes.

🌐 No Installation Required: Runs entirely in your web browser with a single HTML file—no downloads, no app stores, and 100% free.

Who is this for?

Seniors & Visually Impaired: Anyone who gets eye strain from reading the fine print on modern packaging.

Allergy Sufferers: People who need to rapidly check food items for hidden allergens without squinting.

Parents & Conscious Consumers: Anyone who wants to easily read and understand the active/inactive ingredients in the household products and medicines they bring into their home.

Technical Details

MagnifyAll is a lightweight, single-page React application utilizing Tailwind CSS for responsive, accessible styling. It leverages the HTML5-QRCode library for browser-based camera scanning and interfaces with multiple RESTful APIs (Open Food/Beauty/Products Facts and the US FDA API) to fetch real-time product data.
